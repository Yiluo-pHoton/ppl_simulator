Your Aviation Journey Begins

Welcome, future aviator! You've just enrolled in flight training with $15,000 and a dream of earning your Private Pilot License (PPL). This isn't just a game â€“ it's a realistic simulation of what thousands of student pilots experience every year.

The path to your PPL requires 40 flight hours minimum, comprehensive ground knowledge, and passing both written and practical exams. Weather, finances, and your own stamina will all play crucial roles in your success.

Quick Start Guide

Goal: Earn your Private Pilot License in 40+ flight hours

  • Study: Build knowledge (free, builds theory)
  • Fly: Gain flight hours (costs money, weather dependent)
  • Rest: Recover morale and reduce fatigue
